Description:

This assignment was given to me in my APP Academy class. We were assigned to make a map on earthquakes happening all around the world through a Google Maps API, which receives its information through the United States Geological Survey. The USGC uses over 2,500 seismograph stations monitoring earthquakes throughout the United States, Alaska, and. Hawaii. The stations are grouped into regional networks operated by local institutions.Click here for more information.On the map, icons are displayed and when clicked, display the information of the earthquake, such as where it's occuring and the strength of the earthquake.
